History

East-Mec was founded in Arizona in 1914 at the start of WWI so that people could have entertainment while everyone else was at war. It didn't go well and most of the people were made fun of for being cowards that wouldn't fight in the war. We slowly expanded around the area of Arizona and we are now the biggest circus school in the state of Arizona. We are very presltigous and have a beutiful history of successful performers!
